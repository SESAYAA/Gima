Gima Agric SL Ltd

Gima Agric SL Ltd is a premier agricultural company established to engage in comprehensive agribusiness operations, including fish farming, cattle rearing, livestock management, and crop production. The company specializes in harvesting, grading, pooling, handling and marketing agricultural products both locally and internationally.

Mission and Vision

Our Vision is to be a leading and sustainable agricultural enterprise in Sierra Leone and beyond, recognized for high-quality produce, innovative practices and contribution to food security.

  • To Produce high-quality agricultural products using sustainable and efficient farming practices.
  • To contribute to the growth of Sierra Leone agricultural sector through innovation, technology and responsible farming.
  • To establish a strong market place both Locally and Internationally
